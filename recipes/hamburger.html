Hamburger

Description

These hamburgers are a huge improvement on the normal, plain hamburger. I think that is what is making all of those cows so mad! These bad boys leave room for improvisation too; if you don't have all of the ingredients, be creative! Serve on buns with all the fixin's.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, sliced
